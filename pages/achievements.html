Achievements of Linus Torvalds

  • Created the Linux kernel, a cornerstone of the open-source software movement.
  • Received the Millennium Technology Prize in 2012 for his work on Linux.
  • Founded the Git version control system, which has become a standard in software development.
  • Continues to lead the Linux kernel project, with millions of developers contributing worldwide.
